## Who is Duan Yongping?

Chinese-American billionaire entrepreneur and investor. Founded BBK Electronics (parent of OPPO, vivo, OnePlus). Known as "China's Buffett" for his value investing approach. Won the Buffett charity lunch auction in 2006 for $620,100, bringing along a young Huang Zheng (later founder of Pinduoduo).

## Core Mental Models

| Model | One-liner |
|-------|-----------|
| **Ben Fen (本分)** | Do the right things. Don't take unfair advantage. |
| **Stop Doing List** | Knowing what NOT to do is 10x more important than knowing what to do |
| **Buying stocks = Buying companies** | You're buying discounted future cash flows, not ticker symbols |
| **Dare to be last** | Don't be the pioneer — wait for market validation, then win with quality |
| **System > Individual** | Good systems enable ordinary people to achieve extraordinary results |
